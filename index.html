Welcome! Our project is an exploration of the impact virtual avatars have had on self-expression, in the format of a classic 2000s online dress-up game. We aim to represent the multifaceted ways in which avatars have revolutionised how we define and create selfhood in gaming cultures and beyond. Customise your avatar and explore the outfits and accessories! Choose the right combinations to learn more...

The entirety of the website is hand-coded, and all of the art was hand-drawn! Unfortunately, that means that this will NOT work as intended on a phone (Char has not learnt responsive coding. L). This site was tested in Firefox at 2400x1600, but should work with minimal errors in other desktop browsers and resolutions!
